Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical micro-SaaS project takes 3-6 months from start to launch. I provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation phase.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! I specialize in working with startups and early-stage companies. I understand the unique challenges of building an MVP and can help you launch faster.

What technologies do you specialize in?

My primary expertise is in Angular, TypeScript, Node.js, and Firebase. I also work with various AI APIs, cloud platforms (AWS, Firebase), and modern web technologies.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! I offer various support packages including maintenance, feature updates, and technical consulting on a retainer basis after project completion.

How do you handle project communication?

I believe in transparent, regular communication. We'll have weekly check-ins, and I'm available via email, Slack, or video calls throughout the project.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing depending on your needs. Project-based pricing starts at $10,000. I provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements.
